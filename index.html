Hello, It's Me

Parin Shira

And I'm a Security Engineer

I’m a passionate Cybersecurity Analyst specializing in network protection, threat analysis, and phishing attack prevention. With a strong foundation in network security protocols, vulnerability assessment, and incident response, I focus on identifying risks before they turn into real threats. My work involves designing secure network environments, detecting phishing campaigns, and implementing proactive defense mechanisms to protect sensitive data. I have hands-on experience in analyzing security logs, monitoring intrusion attempts, and applying effective countermeasures to safeguard systems. I believe cybersecurity is not just about technology—it’s about trust, awareness, and continuous improvement. My mission is to strengthen digital resilience and create a safer cyberspace through analytical thinking, ethical practices, and technical precision.
